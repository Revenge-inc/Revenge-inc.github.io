SEPTEMBER 2023 RELEASE

Revenge - is a dynamic multiplayer shooter developed by the Ukrainian indie studio Waves Interaction. The game repeats the dynamics of the old games of the late 90s.

Coming soon Trailer

System requirements

    Recommended

  • Processor : Intel Core i7-4930k
  • Graphic card : NVIDIA GeForce GTX 780 Ti or GTX 1060 3GB
  • RAM : 8GB

  • Hard disk space : -

  • Operating System : Windows 10 or 11

We would like to remind you that the game is in the development stage, and it is impossible to specify the final system requirements.

But these indicators give at least an exemplary idea of ​​project optimization.

We will try our best to make the project work even on average computers and laptops.

About the project

Information about the project developed by our studio

The real bayraktar in the game.

There is a real bayraktar in the game, which can help you destroy opponents.

Air alarm

If your opponent is calling reinforcements in the game, you will hear an air alarm.

Lots of weapons

Different and unique weapons to choose from, from automatics and semi-automatics to shotguns, pistols, etc.

The atmosphere of old games of the 90s

If you miss the fast-paced style of old games like Doom and Wolfenstain 3D. What is Revenge the game for you.
